Introduction

In the year 2020, Mrs. N. De Silva and Mrs. R. Uduwela led the college students who wanted to experience diplomatic procedure similar to that of the United Nations to the BMICH in order to participate in Diplomat’s MUN. Afterwards the seniors of our school established the Mahamaya Model United Nations club with the purpose of developing the diplomatic skills of the younger generations of the school and giving them a platform to express their ideas on current global issues. This would also give the opportunity to mold, well-spoken female individuals from our school that could lead society.

This club is open to all students from grades 6-13 who have a passion for diplomacy and politics and who are interested in developing their public speaking skills. We as the MMUN give valuable opportunities to students to attend conferences organized by professionals in the field. We have also taken the initiative to train young MUNers on MUN procedures and their overall oratory skills.

MMUN’22

Workshop

The MMUN club is organizing a workshop in order to educate the students of the schools on the MUN procedure and spread awareness on MUN in order to increase the involvement of students of Kandy schools in the MUN platform.

Model United Nation is a platform that helps develop your communication skills, leadership skills and your knowledge on prevailing global issues.

Thus, we hope this would provide students with encouragement to establish MUN in the schools.

This workshop will be held on the 3rd of December 2022 from 8.30am at the school premises. The workshop will be conducted by an experienced professional in the field.
